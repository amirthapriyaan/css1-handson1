VEGETABLE SALAD

Benefits Of Vegetable Salad

Salad greens contain Vitamin A, Vitamin C, beta-carotene, calcium, folate, fiber, and phytonutrients (see Table 1). Leafy vegetables are a good choice for a healthful diet because they do not contain cholesterol and are naturally low in calories and sodium.



Steps To Make VegetableSalad:

  • * Peel the cucumber and wash in normal water along with the tomato.
  • *Cut the beet root, tomato and cucumber into rings.
  • *Sprinkle some salt and chaat masala.
  • *Serve this nutritional salad with any meal.
